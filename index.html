Projects

I've had the luxury of working on a number of projects ranging from a visualization design platform, a cell microscopy visualization tool, and more personal projects. Most of my software development experience is in Javascript and Python. I focus mainly on single page web applications with React.js front-ends and use Django as my go-to back-end server.

Research

My published research focuses on the intersection of mathematics and exploratory data analysis. More specifically, I worked in he field of Topological Data Analysis (TDA) where I studied metrics between Reeb graphs and merge trees.
